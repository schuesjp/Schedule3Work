Current federal hook

Schedule III rescheduling transition

April 23, 2026 federal action that immediately placed FDA-approved marijuana products and marijuana products subject to a qualifying state-issued medical marijuana license in Schedule III, while a new federal hearing beginning June 29, 2026 addresses the broader proposal to move marijuana more generally from Schedule I to Schedule III.
