Current market angle

The most useful pitch is not “cannabis consulting.” It is sharp written support for people whose work now sits in a messier federal-state transition.

The strongest current hook is the partial federal shift in April 2026 and the continuing hearing process that followed. The old one-size-fits-all assumption that every marijuana question begins and ends with blanket federal illegality does less work now, especially for state-medical questions, tax and business planning, diligence, and internal client-facing analysis. That creates room for scoped research memos that help a client or advisor figure out what is covered, what is still excluded, where ambiguity remains, and what should be escalated to licensed counsel or a tax professional.
