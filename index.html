April 2026 federal update

Official Schedule III move for certain marijuana products.

On April 23, 2026, the Justice Department announced an official federal move placing FDA-approved marijuana products and marijuana products subject to a qualifying state-issued medical marijuana license into Schedule III, while also starting a hearing process on broader marijuana rescheduling.

For people working around cannabis questions, that means the old blanket assumption of across-the-board federal Schedule I treatment is no longer doing the same work in every medical, diligence, advisory, or transition setting.
